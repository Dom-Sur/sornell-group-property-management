3. Quick sort into:
   - Bills to enter

## QuickBooks Entry (1.5 hours)
For each document:

### BILLS/INVOICES:
1. Click **+ New â†’ Bill**
2. Enter:
   - Vendor (create if new)
   - Date from invoice
   - Due date
   - Amount
   - **CLASS: Select property** â† CRITICAL!
   - Category (see quick list below)
   - Attach document
3. Save & Next

### DEPOSITS:
1. Click **+ New â†’ Bank Deposit**
2. Select account: TD Bank or Baselane
3. Enter tenant name
4. Amount
5. **CLASS: Property** â† MUST SELECT!
6. Category: Rental Income

### CREDIT CARDS:
1. Click **+ New â†’ Expense**
2. Select credit card account
3. Enter details
4. **CLASS: Property or Admin**
5. Attach receipt

## End of Session (15 minutes)
1. Move completed docs to `/COMPLETED_ENTRIES/`
2. Move unclear to `/NEEDS_INFO/`
